Whetstone Benchmark Overview

Purpose:

The Whetstone benchmark is intended to measure double precision floating point operations

Additional Information

- Whetstone originally came from Whetstone, Leicestershire England.
- Whetstone is small enough to fit entirely into L1 cache

Input: number of loops
Output: Millions of Whetstone Instructions per Second / MWIPS

Warning:
If your running on a Beaglebone White/Black please plug in the 5V Power Supply.
Otherwise, the board may shutdown or reboot due to using to much current from USB.
