Dhrystone Benchmark Overview

Purpose:

The Dhrystone benchmark is intended to give a raw processor speed number indicating how many dhrystones can be executed in one scecond.

Additional Information

- Dhrystone contains no floating point operations
- Dhrystone is small enough to fit entirely into L1 cache

Input: (Number of Iterations) (CPU clock speed in KHz)
Output: Dhrystones per Second / DMIPS/MHz

Warning:
If your running on a Beaglebone White/Black please plug in the 5V Power Supply.
Otherwise, the board may shutdown or reboot due to using to much current from USB.
