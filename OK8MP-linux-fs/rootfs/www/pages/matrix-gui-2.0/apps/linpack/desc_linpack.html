Linpack Benchmark Overview

Purpose:

The Linpack benchmark measures the performance of single precision floating point operations

Additional Information

- Linpack was originally inteded for use on supercomputers in the 1970s
- Linpack is small enough to fit entirely into L1 cache

Input: None
Output: Thousands of Floating Point Operations per Second / KFLOPS

Warning:
If your running on a Beaglebone White/Black please plug in the 5V Power Supply.
Otherwise, the board may shutdown or reboot due to using to much current from USB.
